Frequently Asked Questions

Curious about your bank? Find answers to the most common questions from our community of Filipino banking clients and see how SatisBank empowers your financial decisions.

What is the source of the customer satisfaction data?

SatisBank gathers data from a combination of public surveys, sentiment analysis of social media and news feeds, and proprietary user-submitted ratings. All data is aggregated and anonymized to calculate the **SatisScore** and other core metrics.

How often are the bank comparison data updated?

We pride ourselves on providing a data where possible. Interest rates and fee schedules are checked and updated semiannually. Customer satisfaction scores are updated semiannually. Major product features and regulatory changes are updated within a week of official announcement.

Can I compare more than two banks at once?

Yes, our advanced comparison tool allows you to select as many as you want.

What is the difference between an Existing Bank Client and a Prospective Client Profile?

The **Existing Client Profile** uses comparison metrics relevant to current account holders (e.g., overdraft fees, loyalty programs, online banking features). The **Prospective Client Profile** focuses on metrics relevant to switching or opening a new account (e.g., introductory offers, sign-up bonuses, ease of application).
